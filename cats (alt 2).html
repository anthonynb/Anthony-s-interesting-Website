I (kind of)

like cats!




Cats are great... when their not furry little nightmares. Like I said, I (kind of) like cats, anyway, I have two
sisters that have five cats total at my place. Even with the fact that I like cats and all I thought we had enough
at two, but no, two more furry little demons were brought in to the house
no questions asked and took over the land as far as
the eye can see. But still, I adore the fluffy little bugs!

  7. Control their human and wake their human up with meowing and sitting human.
